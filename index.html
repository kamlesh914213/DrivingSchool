How You Learn

Learn how to get your driver's license in Ontario.

STEP

01

Get Your G1 License

Be 16+ years old, pass a vision test and a written test on road rules. Receive your G1 license and start Level One.

STEP

02

Move to G2 License

Wait 12 months (or 8 months with a BDE course). Pass your first road test to get your G2 license.

STEP

03

Get Your Full G License

After gaining experience, pass your second road test to earn your full G license. The process takes at least 20 months.
